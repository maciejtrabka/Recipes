Apple Pie Cake

Description

A delightful fusion of classic apple pie and moist cake layers, creating a delicious and visually appealing dessert.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups peeled, sliced apples (such as Granny Smith)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups packed brown sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup milk

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. In a bowl, combine the sliced apples, granulated sugar, and cinnamon. Set aside.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the butter and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients.
  6. Spread half of the batter into the prepared baking dish. Arrange the apple mixture evenly over the batter. Top with the remaining batter.
  7. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Allow the pie cake to cool before serving. Optionally, dust with powdered sugar or serve with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
